Laura

The legend says that if you put Laura in a room with 100 people, she will make 101 friends.

From english teacher to goat farmer, Laura knows it all. She lived 15 years in Spain, 4 years in England, and she'll live the rest of the days with Radu wherever they want.

Besides her ability to find innovative solutions to every problem, she has an almost super-human sense of smell.

With her inexhaustible energy, Laura has always been the life of the party. A people magnet that advocates for social causes, always seeing the goodness in people and refusing to be influenced by prejudices.

But Laura doesn't love only humnas, but animals too and everything that has life. She's enamored with nature and she's absolutely fascinated by sunsets and sunrises.

All these things make Laura a true force of nature. An incorruptible ally of truth who is not interested in material possesions, choosing instead to be anchored in meaningful values that us, normal people, are rarely able or willing to observe.

Radu

They say that if you ask Radu for help, he will prepare a 3 part presentation. I hope you are prepared to take notes and are a good listener.

He is extremely dedicated to whatever he put his mind to, but he is stoic enough to realise that what's in God's hands doesn't deserve stressing over.

At first glance, he may seem withdrawn or in his own world, and sometimes he is, but the seemingly silent inattention is most often a complex process of introspection, analysis, and full presence.

Radu has seen two continents, he is the ace up the sleeve for a multinational company, he taught himself to play bass, he wrote every line of code on the website you are on, and yet, his greatest achievement is that he is marrying his first love.

The perfect weekend for him is a walk in the mountains or the vibrations of bass strings after a small sugar-free expresso.

He doesn't know how to do everything, but he doesn't shy away from anything. That's how, in just one summer, he gained experience as a farmer, adoptive father to 3 cats and 2 puppies, web developer, and much more. The only thing he absolutely refuses to do is eat spinach soup with fried pork.

On another note, Radu is the calmest and most serene person you could meet. His essential calmness, which is Laura's soothing presence, can also make you laugh until you cry. The comedic effect is amplified by his neutral expression and semi-serious tone.
